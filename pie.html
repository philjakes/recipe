Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

one-9 inch pie
30 min-prep time
10 min-cooking time
40 min-total
8 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust,baked
  • 4 eggs whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium sourcepan ...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch and salt.
    • Stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-height,stirring frequently, untill mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • Bring to boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass or meat bowl..
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over pie,sealimg the edges at the crust
  4. Bake:Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or untill meringue is golden brown.
